PayRisk AI — Receivables and Payment Reminder Software

PayRisk AI is a software company with a direct SaaS offering for small businesses. Merchants use PayRisk AI to track receivables, assess payment risk, send transactional payment reminders, and understand customer repurchase intent.

SMS account-notification program

PayRisk AI sends transactional account-notification SMS initiated by merchants to end customers who have opted in. Messages include invoice and balance reminders, due-date notices, overdue notices, and payment confirmations. Messages are not marketing or promotional. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.
