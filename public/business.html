Public business information

PayRisk AI helps small businesses manage receivables and payment follow-up.

PayRisk AI is a software company with a direct SaaS offering at payriskai.com. The platform helps merchants track amounts owed, assess payment risk, send payment reminders, and identify customer repurchase opportunities.

Receivables tracking

Manage customer balances, invoices, due dates, payment status, and transaction history.

Payment reminders

Send transactional payment and account reminders by email and SMS.

Risk and value insights

Prioritize follow-up using payment-risk scores and repurchase-intent insights.

Business and audience

PayRisk AI serves businesses that extend payment terms or maintain customer receivables. Merchants use the platform to manage their own customer records. SMS recipients are customers or business contacts who have expressly opted in to receive payment and account-related text messages from their merchant via PayRisk AI.

SMS messaging program

Sender: PayRisk AI sends transactional account-notification SMS initiated by merchants using the PayRisk AI platform. Each message identifies PayRisk AI and may reference the relevant merchant.

Recipients: End customers who have opted in through a website or digital form using an unchecked-by-default SMS consent checkbox.

Purpose: Invoice and balance reminders, upcoming due-date notices, overdue payment notices, and payment confirmations. Messages are not marketing or promotional.

Frequency and rates: Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out and help: Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help, or email info@payriskai.com.

Consent and public compliance information

Before any SMS is sent, the end user must opt in and the merchant must confirm that consent in PayRisk AI. The public SMS opt-in page shows the complete consumer consent language and a screenshot of the platform's unchecked merchant-attestation control.
