SMS Opt-In / Call to Action

Public documentation for US A2P 10DLC campaign review · Last updated: July 14, 2026

PayRisk AI is a business software platform. Merchants use PayRisk AI to send payment and account-related text messages (invoice reminders, due-date notices, overdue balance notices) to their own customers. Messages are transactional / account notifications — not marketing promotions. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

PayRisk AI does not use a text-in keyword opt-in. End users consent with the merchant. Before any SMS is sent, the merchant must confirm that consent in PayRisk AI.

1. Primary opt-in method — website / digital form

End users opt in on the merchant’s website, customer portal, digital intake form, contract, or invoice by providing a mobile number and checking an unchecked-by-default consent box. The customer can complete the form without checking the box; SMS is not enabled unless they actively opt in.

2. Platform enforcement (before any SMS is sent)

After the merchant obtains consent from the customer, the merchant adds the phone number in PayRisk AI. The merchant must check an unchecked-by-default attestation box. SMS cannot be sent without this confirmation.

3. Opt-out and help

  • Reply STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for help, or email info@payriskai.com.

4. Privacy and mobile number sharing

Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in data are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Full details: https://payriskai.com/privacy.
